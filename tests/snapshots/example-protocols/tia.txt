Urgent evaluation and risk reduction for transient ischemic attack
Disposition is risk-stratified: admit/observe high-risk TIAs, and use rapid outpatient pathways only when same-day workup plus reliable 24-48h stroke follow-up are guaranteed. Do not use ABCD2 alone.

Antiplatelet Therapy — DAPT Protocol
High-Risk TIA or Minor Stroke (NIHSS ≤3) — CHANCE/POINT Protocol
Day 1 (Loading)
ASA 325 mg + Clopidogrel 300 mg
Days 2-21
ASA 81 mg + Clopidogrel 75 mg daily
Day 22+
Mono antiplatelet (ASA 81 or Clop 75)
CYP2C19 Testing:
Order CYP2C19 genotyping. Poor metabolizers (*2/*2, *2/*3, *3/*3) have reduced clopidogrel efficacy — switch to ticagrelor 90mg BID.
If AF identified:
Switch from DAPT to anticoagulation (DOAC preferred). Do NOT combine long-term DAPT + anticoagulation.
