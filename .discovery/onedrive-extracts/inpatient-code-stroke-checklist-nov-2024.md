PATIENT WITH STROKE SYMPTOMS NOT SEEN/DOCUMENTED PREVIOUSLY
Neurologic symptoms with a last known well ≤24 hours that are:
✓  FOCAL (e.g. unilateral weakness, language/speech disturbance, significant vision change, abrupt onset coma,

         non- seizure/drug-induced sudden loss of consciousness)

✓  DISABLING (e.g. a deficit that would prevent performance of basic activities of daily living or returning to work--

 would the deficit impair the patient's abilities to perform activities of daily living?)

✓  NEW (seen without symptoms within ≤24 hours)

•  RN, APPs, MD: Identify new BEFAST symptom that is new, focal, and disabling
