# PULSARA – Stroke Call Responsibilities

Log-in when on call
Acute reperfusion therapy
Use "mix TNK. ED, confirm order placed" pre-typed message when recommending thrombolytic therapy
Toggle "suspected LVO" (to YES) early when a proximal anterior circulation or basilar artery LVO is evident on imaging
End case with a summary that includes patient presenting symptoms, NIHSS, CT/CTA findings, reperfusion treatment recommendation (& rationale)
Transfer cases:
List name as "Z(First Name) Z(Last Name)"
Add MRN (when available)
In PULSARA communication, including the following:
Imaging planned after arrival to HMC
ETA from OSH (provided by transfer center)
Patient family/LNOK name + phone number
Intubation, hemodynamic instability, trauma, or isolation precautions (if applicable)
