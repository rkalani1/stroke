**Acute Stroke Evaluation and Treatment**
UW Medicine Comprehensive Stroke Center at Harborview
www.stroke.washington.edu

## Trigger

If persistent / fluctuating and potentially disabling stroke symptoms < 24 hrs:
Call **CODE STROKE**, start **PULSARA** case (start case before ED arrival if pre-notification).

## ED Staff Key Elements (First 15 minutes in ER)

- Meet patient at door (if pre-notification)
- Brief history and exam: document time last known well, NIHSS, GCS
- DIRECT TO STAT HEAD CT (non-contrast and CTA)
  - Contrast allergy protocol (linked)
- Weigh patient on transfer to scanner table
- STAT blood draw: full electrolytes/glucose, CBC, Emergency Stroke Panel (ESP)
- Place 2 large-bore peripheral IVs (avoid Foley catheter placement if able)

## Imaging triage gate (~15 min from arrival)

Radiology, Neurology, ER review CT imaging:
**Blood on Head CT?**

### YES — Blood (IPH/SAH)
CTA Head/Neck (run thru circle of Willis)

#### Intraparenchymal Hemorrhage (IPH)
- Neurology Resident calls Stroke Phone to discuss case, including role for minimally invasive evacuation in select IPH
- Neurology consults Neurosurgery if severe mass effect, lobar, hydrocephalus, cerebellar, IPH; co-morbid SAH, for joint discussion with surgery
- Document ICH score
- BP Control (linked)
- Review Guide for Reversing Coagulopathies (linked)
- Admit to Neurology/NCCS in most cases
- EPIC IPH Acute Care (floor) order set or EPIC IPH NICU order set

#### Subarachnoid Hemorrhage (SAH)
- Consult Neurosurgery
- Document Hunt and Hess score
- Swallow Screen
- Review Guide for Reversing Coagulopathies (linked)
- Neurosurgery will notify Vascular Neurosurgery attending; NCCS for admission
- EPIC NSurg/NCCS SAH order set

### NO — Blood (Ischemic)
CTA Neck/Head (work thru circle of Willis)

#### Time last known well (LKW) ≥ 4 hrs, 15 min ago?

**No** → **IV Thrombolysis Candidate**
- For eligible candidates, call Stroke Phone early & inform Pharmacy to mix TNK via PULSARA. (ER) Place order for thrombolytic in EPIC.
- Give IV push information form to patient/family to review; document consent
- Review inclusion/exclusion criteria
- SAFETY PAUSE before administration: if patient qualifies, START TNK
  - Admit to ICU for ≥ 24 h
  - Complications: Possible ICH (linked); Angioedema (management)
- Document NIHSS score
- BP Control (linked)
- EPIC Ischemic Stroke NCU Thrombolytic orders

**Yes** → continue to LVO assessment

#### Large vessel occlusion (ICA, MCA, basilar) and LKW to HMC arrival ≤ 24 hrs ago?

- **No LVO** or **No LVO 6-24 h** → "Stroke Code MRI" or CTP for mismatch
- **Yes LVO** → **Endovascular Thrombectomy Candidate**
  - Call Stroke Phone
  - Engage Neuro-Interventional team
  - Give Interventional IRx information form to patient/family to review (interventional team does final consent)
  - Review inclusion/exclusion criteria
  - If eligible, neuro-interventional team consents, and admit to NCU for 24h post-EVT
  - Document NIHSS score
  - BP Control (linked)
  - Timing of dual-antiplatelets if stent during EVT

## Closeout

**Cancel Stroke Code OR Close Pulsara Case Post-Intervention(s)**
- Swallow Screen
- Initiate corresponding EPIC admission order set
