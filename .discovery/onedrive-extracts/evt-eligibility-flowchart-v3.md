### Footnotes
- *LVO = ICA or proximal M1 MCA*
- *‡ Dominant proximal M2 MCA occlusion: M2 branch ≥ 1 cm from MCA bifurcation, supplying ≥ 50% MCA territory*
- *DISTAL trial MD criteria: codominant tissue at risk ≤ 100 mL; codominant M2: 60-100 mL, no mismatch; M2 < 80 mL*
- *Recommendations assume baseline mRS 0-1 unless otherwise specified*
- *mRS (modified Rankin Scale, baseline pre-stroke): 0 — no symptoms; 1 — no significant disability; 2 — slight disability; 3 — moderate, walks unaided; 4 — moderately severe, needs assistance; 5 — severe, bedridden; 6 — dead*
- *Generalizability limited for: age > 90, significant headwind reverse tortuosity, comorbidities affecting assessment, seizures at stroke onset, high suspicion for underlying ICAD*
