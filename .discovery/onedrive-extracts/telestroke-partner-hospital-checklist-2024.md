Checklist for Telestroke Consult Requests

# Procedure

* Identify need for Telestroke consultation (patient with stroke symptoms, <4.5hr from LKW or unclear
* **Call** the Transfer Center stroke phone **(206) 520-7580** and provide the following information:
  + Pt
    - Name
    - DOB
    - Sex
  + DX (say “**stroke code**”) & request stroke MD
  + Referring Physician full name
    - Contact Info for referring MD
    - Contact Info for ED Front Desk (if different from MD)
* **Fax** patient’s **face sheet** (demographics) to **(206) 520-3252**
* **Push** radiology **images** (non-con CT first, followed by CTA & MRI) to HMC Radiology
* **Provider will be conferenced w/ HMC** Stroke Neurologist, have the following information ready:
  + Basic Assessment
  + Last known well time
  + Patient History (esp. recent surgery, bleeding, DOAC use, stroke)
* If your patient is going to be **transferred**, the **Transfer Center will call you back** to:
  + **Verify** which **hospital location** you are sending the patient to
  + Request an **ETA**
  + Request a **call if there are changes** in patient status or ETA
* Arrange fastest possible transport (usually air)
* Update transfer center with changes in patient status/condition (intubation, neuro changes, etc)

\*Reminder-send updated face sheet if initial fax is incomplete

**Checklist for Telestroke Consults**

1. Acute stroke symptoms/signs + last known normal ≤4.5 hours (or not known) 🡪 call “stroke code” consult with stroke neurologist
   1. Call HMC transfer center 206-520-7580
   2. Provide: patient name, DOB, sex + referring physician name & contact information
   3. Fax patient face sheet 206-520-3252
   4. Push radiology imaging (CT, CTA head/neck, MRI Brain) to HMC radiology
2. Provide stroke MD (discuss available information):
   1. Neurologic symptoms/signs + age, medical history, medication list
   2. Current BP
   3. Exam – focal deficits
   4. Blood glucose, platelets, & coagulation studies (INR, aPTT)
   5. Head CT & CTA head/neck findings
3. If TNK candidate (no ICH, no clear contraindication) 🡪 connect to video

If not TNK candidate 🡪 LVO on CTA? 🡪 if LVO, transfer to HMC; if no LVO, discuss triage and evaluation/mgmt. recommendations by phone

* Arrange fastest possible transfer (air)
* Callback stroke MD with new information OR clinical change
