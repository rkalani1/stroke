IPD meta-analysis — anticoagulation timing after ischaemic stroke with AF
AF anticoag timing Lancet 2025 New to Atlas verified

Early DOAC (≤4 d) cut the 30-day composite — OR 0.70 (95% CI 0.50–0.98). Pooled 5,441 patients across 4 RCTs. Supports not delaying.

LASTE
Thrombectomy for stroke with a large infarct of unrestricted size
Acute · large core NEJM 2024 New to Atlas verified

EVT benefits even very large infarcts (ASPECTS ≤5) — generalized OR 1.63 (1.29–2.06); mortality 36% vs 56% — at the cost of more sICH.

Apixaban vs aspirin in cryptogenic stroke with atrial cardiopathy

No benefit — HR 1.00 (0.64–1.55), stopped for futility. Atrial cardiopathy alone does not justify anticoagulation.

Endovascular treatment of acute basilar-artery occlusion
Acute · basilar EVT NEJM 2022 New topic verified

EVT benefits basilar occlusion — good outcome 46% vs ~23% (≤12 h) and 46% vs 24% (6–24 h). Establishes posterior-circulation thrombectomy.
